Regex Explanation:
\d allows digits ranging from 0-9
{m, n} matches the preceding statement as many times as possible from m to n
Jan(?:uary) accepts either jan or january
? is the same as {0, 1}
?: means that the group isn't captured so it can't be referenced later
| means OR
[\/\\\-\. ] accepts any of the characters in the range including <space>. Some characters are escaped.
[]| means or <nothing>
